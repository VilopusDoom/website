Who am I?

I'm an aspiring Cyber-Security professional studying in Ontario, Canada

From an early age, I've always enjoyed computers(and their games, especially their games). I always found myself playing the defensive role in computer games. In Real-Time Strategy games, I would 'bunker' - outlast my opponents. In Role-Playing games, I would play defensive characters such as the Paladin, or Tank. This affinity to outlasting potential opposition helped me to choose a security-oriented profession. Defend the Weak, and all that guff. It was in high-school, Grade 12 to be specific, that I fell in love with computers, and their inner workings. After trying(and failing) to maintain a stable Arch Linux install, I installed Fedora Linux, as a dual boot to my native Windows 10. I then used Fedora for the majority of my Senior High-School year, as well as my first, and only semester at the University of Toronto - Mississauga, Computer Science. This tinkering with Linux led me to more openly consider a career in Computers. Bolstered by my affinity for defensive play-styles, I chose to harmonize defense with computers, and enrolled in an Information Systems Security Degree.
